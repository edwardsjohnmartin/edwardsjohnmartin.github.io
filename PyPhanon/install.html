PyPhanon

Instructions for installing the PyPhanon plugin

  1. Open PyCharm preferences.
  2. Select "Plugins" on the left.
  3. Select "Marketplace" in the top center.
  4. Enter "PyPhanon" in the search field.
  5. Click "Install".
  6. Restart PyCharm when prompted.
  7. After restarting and pressing a key you will see an informed consent form. Fill out the form. If you fill out the form you will receive the extra credit regardless of whether you agree to participate in the study or not.
